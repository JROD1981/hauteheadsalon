Frequently Asked Questions

How do I schedule an appointment?
Please call the Salon at (817) 615-9680 during open hours to schedule your appointment

Depending on the service you are scheduled for it may take 30 minutes for just a haircut but up to or more than hour and half for specialty services
What can I expect from my service provider during my salon treatment?
You can expect a warm welcoming and quality service. We understand that waiting for a haircut can be lingering but we provide a coffee bar and charging stations for waiting clients
Walk ins accepted?
